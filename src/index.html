The

UNHOLY TRINITY

The Unholy Trinity is a dark embodiment of chaos and power. It represents the three primal forces of corruption, decay, and doom – manifesting in forms that haunt mortal minds. Their whispers weave curses, their presence decays beauty, and their touch brings oblivion. They gather under the blood moon, performing forbidden rites to sustain their eternal dominion over the shadows.

  • Corruption
  • Decay
  • Doom

Corruption seeps into the hearts of mortals, twisting their desires and enslaving their will to the void. It is the first of the Unholy Trinity, born from the endless abyss, forever consuming purity and hope with its vile embrace.

The Ritual

Their summoning requires forbidden rituals, spoken in tongues long lost to mankind. The gathering shadows form their shape, revealing fleshless visages and burning crimson eyes. They stand in silent unity, each a pillar of dread. Their chant is felt rather than heard, vibrating in the bones of those who dare witness. Their presence chills the air, distorts reality, and binds the realm in night eternal. The Unholy Trinity thrives on fear and feeds upon the souls of the fallen, granting forbidden knowledge in return for eternal servitude.
